# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Shape Survivor is designed to be playable offline.

## Data We Collect

Shape Survivor does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The game stores progress locally on your device, including:

- best time
- best kills
- earned cores
- workshop upgrades
- settings
- core unlocks
- mission and achievement progress

This data stays on your device unless you delete the app, clear browser storage, back up your device, or use platform-level sync features outside the app.

## Network Use

The game does not require an internet connection for gameplay. The web build includes offline caching so the game can continue working after it has loaded once.

## Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

Shape Survivor does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

## Children

Shape Survivor does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Platform Services

If you install or download the app through Apple App Store or another platform, that platform may process information according to its own privacy policy. Those platform services are outside Shape Survivor's control.
